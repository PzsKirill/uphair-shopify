The clinical file

Numbers from a lab, not a marketing deck.

Three independent trials. 142 participants total. 84 days of consistent use, instrumental measurement at baseline, day 30, day 60 and day 84. No retouch, no rounding up, no "results may vary" small print to hide behind.

+38% Average increase in active follicles (phototrichogram, n=48, day 84)

92% Of subjects reported visible regrowth at day 90 (consumer panel, n=46)
0 Adverse reactions reported across the three trials (dermatology review, n=142)
Methodology, in plain English

Three randomised, controlled studies run by an independent CRO (Eurofins Cosmetic & Personal Care, Hamburg). Subjects 28–58, mixed hair types, no concurrent treatments. Measurement via phototrichogram (counted follicles), TrichoScan (strand diameter), and standardised consumer perception questionnaire.

What we didn't measure

We don't claim uphair treats androgenetic alopecia, regrows hair on fully closed follicles, or replaces medical care. It's a cosmetic serum that supports the appearance of fuller hair — and the data above is what that means in numbers.

    Frequently asked

    Everything you might want to know before you commit.

    Can't find your question? Our support team is two real humans who read every message. help@uphair.shop

    How long until I see results?
    Most people notice less shedding and a calmer scalp within 14 days. Visible thickness shows up around day 30. New baby hairs along the hairline typically appear by day 60–90. We recommend the 3-bottle protocol because that's the timeline we measured in our trials — and the one our happiest customers complete.
    Is it safe to use every day?
    Yes. uphair is dermatologist-tested and formulated without sulfates, parabens, silicones, synthetic fragrance, or alcohol. The actives are dosed for nightly use. Most people apply 8–10 drops to a clean, dry scalp before bed and massage in for 30 seconds.
    Will my hair fall out if I stop?
    No. Unlike pharmaceutical treatments that suppress hormones, uphair works by waking dormant follicles and strengthening the strands you grow naturally. Once a follicle is re-activated, it stays in the growth cycle. Most customers reduce to 3 nights a week for maintenance after month four.
    Does it work for men too?
    Yes. The mechanism — peptide signaling, microcirculation, scalp-barrier repair — is identical regardless of sex. About 32% of our customers identify as male. Hereditary pattern baldness with fully closed follicles is the one case where progress is much slower; we recommend talking to a trichologist alongside using the serum.
    Can I use it on color-treated or relaxed hair?
    Absolutely. uphair is applied directly to the scalp, not the lengths, and contains no oxidative or stripping agents. It's safe with permanent color, balayage, keratin treatments, and chemical relaxers. We've color-fastness-tested at three independent labs.
    What if it doesn't work for me?
    Take the full 90 days. If you don't see a visible difference, email us with your before/after photos and we'll refund every penny, even if you've used all three bottles. We can stand behind this because the data has us covered — but the guarantee is yours.
